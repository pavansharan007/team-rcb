About RCB

Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is one of the most popular and passionate franchises in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, RCB represents the vibrant city of Bengaluru, Karnataka, and plays its home matches at the iconic M. Chinnaswamy Stadium. Known for its explosive batting line-ups, star-studded squads, and fiercely loyal fanbase, RCB has become a cultural phenomenon in the IPL. Despite not yet winning an IPL title, the team has made multiple playoff and final appearances and continues to be a major contender every season. With legends like Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and modern stars like Faf du Plessis, Glenn Maxwell, and Mohammed Siraj, RCB is a team driven by passion, fire, and the belief that "Ee Sala Cup Namde"
